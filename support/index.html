Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?

A: No. HomeFix works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. No internet connection is required for any feature.

Q: Can I sync my data between devices?

A: Currently, all data is stored locally on the device where you enter it. iCloud sync is not available in version 1.0 but may be considered for future updates.

Q: How do I get started quickly?

A: Go to the Settings tab and tap "Import Template Tasks." This loads 20+ pre-built seasonal maintenance tasks covering HVAC, plumbing, electrical, exterior, landscaping, safety, and appliances. You can then customize or add your own tasks.

Q: How do I add a maintenance task?

A: Go to the Tasks tab and tap the + button. Enter the task name, category (HVAC, Plumbing, etc.), season, frequency (weekly to annually), and any notes. The app will calculate the next due date automatically.

Q: How do I add an appliance?

A: Go to the Appliances tab and tap +. Enter the appliance name, brand, model number, serial number, purchase date, and warranty period. The app calculates the warranty expiry date and tracks repair history.

Q: How do I log a completed task?

A: Swipe left on any task and tap "Complete," or tap into the task detail and mark it done. You can add a maintenance log with date, cost, contractor info, and notes.

Q: What do the status indicators mean?

A: Each task shows a colored dot: green = on track, orange = due within 7 days, red = overdue. The Tasks tab also shows an "Attention Needed" section at the top for overdue tasks.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Absolutely. HomeFix does not collect, transmit, or share any data. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking.

Q: What iOS version is required?

A: HomeFix requires iOS 17.0 or later.

Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?

A: Please email us at support@example.com with a description of the issue or your feature suggestion. Include your iOS version and app version if possible.
