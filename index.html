Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

TL;DR: HomeFix does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your home maintenance data stays on your device. No network access, no accounts, no tracking.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how HomeFix ("the App") handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information.
  • No location data: The App does not access or track your location.
  • No usage analytics: We do not track how you use the App.
  • No crash diagnostics: We do not collect crash reports or diagnostic data.
  • No third-party SDKs: The App contains no third-party tracking or analytics libraries.

3. Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Maintenance Tasks: Task names, categories (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, etc.), seasons, frequencies, due dates, completion history, and notes.
  • Maintenance Logs: Completion dates, costs, contractor names, contractor phone numbers, and notes.
  • Appliances: Appliance names, brands, model numbers, serial numbers, purchase dates, warranty periods, warranty expiry dates, and notes.
  • Repair Records: Repair descriptions, dates, costs, and contractor information linked to specific appliances.

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

The App does not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. There is no network communication of any kind. Your home maintenance data remains entirely on your device.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it benefits from the built-in security features of iOS, including device encryption and app sandboxing. We recommend keeping your device protected with a passcode or biometric authentication.

6. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the App at any time.
  • Modify: You can edit any entry directly within the App.
  • Delete: You can delete individual entries or clear all data by deleting the App from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any personal data from any users, this policy applies universally.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
