Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

HomeMender ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our home maintenance tracking application.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

HomeMender is designed with your privacy in mind. We do NOT:

  • Collect any personal information from you
  • Track your location or usage patterns
  • Access your contacts, photos, or files
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share information with third parties

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create in HomeMender is stored locally on your device only:

  • Maintenance task records and logs
  • Appliance information you enter
  • Notification preferences
  • Usage statistics (stored locally)

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

4. Third-Party Services

HomeMender does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs. The app operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches during transmission.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all your HomeMender data:

  • Delete the app from your device - this removes all stored data immediately
  • No data is retained on any server because we don't have servers

7. Children's Privacy

HomeMender can be used by individuals of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, we don't need parental consent mechanisms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
